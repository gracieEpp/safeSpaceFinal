Statistics and Facts

we're all in this together

Anxiety and depression are the world's most common mental disorders but not many people acknowledge that teens can also be battling these issues. Some will brush it off as the teen looking for attention or ust being a moody teenager. Because they don't pay attention to these things they miss the warning signs.

Anxiety

Anxiety affects 25.1% of teens between the ages of 13 and 18 years old. It is equally as common in children as it is in adults. It is a highly treatable disorder but only 36.9% will recieve the treatment they need. Anxiety can develop from a multitude of factors including genetics, brain chemistry, personality, and life events. Nearly 1/5 of people who are diagnosed with depression are also diagnosed with anxeity.

Depression

Depression affects about 20% of teens before they reach adulthood. That number is greater than that of an adult.Every 100 minutes itsestimated that a teen takes their life and suicide is the third leading cause of death for people between 15 and 24 years old. Only 30% of teens will get treatment for their depression.
